\chapter{Project milestones}

Each milestone is intended to be trained end-to-end and scored on a gauntlet-style evaluation.

\section{Milestone 1 --- Proof of concept}
Fixed six-Pok\'{e}mon team for the agent; opponent teams sampled from the simulator (e.g.\ random battle). Tests whether a policy can exploit a known team against highly variable opponents.

\section{Milestone 2 --- Random vs.\ random}
Both sides use random team generation. Stress-tests representation learning and exploration under high entropy.

\section{Milestone 3 --- Team building}
One agent composes teams; a second (or shared trunk) battles with that team. Combinatorial team space makes this the hardest milestone.

\section{Milestone 4 --- Gauntlet master}
Full trainer gauntlet with strong competence and robustness---the integration target for BDSP structured data.
